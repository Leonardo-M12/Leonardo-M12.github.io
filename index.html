Welcome to my website!

I'm Leonardo, an Applied Mathematics major at the Illinois Institute of Technology in Chicago, and I come from Chitre, Panama.

I'm the Secretary of the Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics (SIAM) Student Chapter at Illinois Tech, where I write the chapter's weekly newsletter. I'm currently doing research on graph theory, specifically in list coloring, one of the many generalizations of the classical notion of graph coloring. I have also taught as an associate instructor for the math olympiad training program back in Panama, where I myself participated as a contestant during high school.
